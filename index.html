Qutub minar


The Qutub Minar, also spelled as Qutb Minar and Qutab Minar, is a minaret and victory tower.The Qutub Minar is a towering 73 meter high tower built by Qutub-ud-Din Aibak in 1193. The tower was built to celebrate Muslim dominance in Delhi after the defeat of Delhi's last Hindu ruler. This tower is the highest tower in India, complete with five storeys and projecting balconies.
